HELP & COMMUNITY SUPPORT

Frequently Asked Questions

Is OmniCart AI completely free to use?

Yes! OmniCart AI is 100% free for consumers. We scan e-commerce platforms to help you find the lowest prices and best seller trust scores without any subscription fees.

How does the 'Buy Now' redirection work?

When you click ⚡ Buy Now on any platform (e.g., Meesho, Flipkart, or Amazon), OmniCart AI opens the exact product search/listing page directly on that official merchant website in a new tab.

Can I save items in my shopping cart across sessions?

Yes! Clicking 🛒 Add to Cart stores your selected items persistently in our Express.js + MongoDB database backend, so your cart is preserved even if you refresh or return later.

Which shopping platforms are supported?

OmniCart AI compares 9 major Indian e-commerce networks: Amazon India, Flipkart, Croma, Reliance Digital, JioMart, Vijay Sales, Myntra, Ajio, and Meesho.
